About this product

Nutritionally, pineapple jalapeño pizza offers a balance of carbohydrates from the crust, protein from any meat toppings, and vitamins and minerals from the vegetables and fruit. While it can be a tasty option, it's essential to be mindful of portion sizes and toppings to keep it balanced. For a simple pineapple jalapeño pizza recipe, you'll need pizza dough, pizza sauce, shredded mozzarella cheese, diced pineapple, sliced jalapeños, and optionally cooked bacon or ham for added flavor.
